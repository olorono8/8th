🎯 Class 8 NCERT Study Plan

Complete and smart study plan to help you become a topper in Class 8!

🗓️ Weekly Time Table

Day Morning Evening Notes
Monday Maths Science Practice problems
Tuesday English Social Science Focus on writing + maps
Wednesday Science Hindi Concepts + grammar
Thursday Maths English Writing & Lit.
Friday Social Science Hindi History / Civics
Saturday Science Maths Practice weak areas
Sunday 📚 Weekly Revision + Mock Tests Revise & test yourself

📅 Monthly Subject Focus

  • Month 1–2: Complete all chapters (Reading + Notes)
  • Month 3–4: Revise and solve NCERT exercises
  • Month 5: Practice with sample papers
  • Month 6: Full syllabus revision + Mock Tests

📌 Subject-Wise Tips

  • Maths: Daily practice, revise formulas, do 2 sample papers/month
  • Science: Revise definitions, draw diagrams, flashcards
  • Social Science: Timeline for history, map marking, real-life Civics
  • English: Reading aloud, writing letters, summaries
  • Hindi: Grammar practice + learn short answers

✅ Daily Checklist

📖 Read NCERT Chapter⬜
✍️ Solve Exercises⬜
🧠 Revise Notes⬜
🔄 Flashcards / Keywords⬜
🧪 Practice Questions⬜
🗓️ Mark Completed on Calendar⬜

🎁 Topper Tips

  • 🎯 Stick to the NCERT book – it's the main key to scoring high
  • 📅 Make a calendar and track your progress
  • 🧠 Teach others – helps you learn faster
  • 🚫 Avoid distractions (no phone during study)
  • 🧘 Stay calm, take breaks and believe in yourself!
