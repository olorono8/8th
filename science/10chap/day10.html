üîé Introduction

Adolescence is the transitional period between childhood and adulthood during which a person undergoes many physical, mental, and emotional changes.

üß¨ Changes at Adolescence

During adolescence, boys and girls experience many changes, called secondary sexual characteristics, which prepare the body for reproduction.

üßë Changes in Boys

  • Increase in height and weight.
  • Development of facial, chest, and pubic hair.
  • Change in voice (voice deepens).
  • Growth of muscles and broader shoulders.
  • Appearance of acne and increased sweating.
  • Testes and penis grow in size.

üëß Changes in Girls

  • Increase in height and weight.
  • Development of breasts.
  • Growth of pubic and underarm hair.
  • Widening of hips.
  • Menstruation (periods) begin.
  • Appearance of acne and increased sweating.

ü©∏ Menstruation in Girls

Menstruation is the monthly discharge of blood and other materials from the uterus of a girl or woman, indicating the ability to reproduce.

It usually starts between 11 to 15 years of age and continues till menopause (~50 years).

üß† Role of Hormones

Hormones are chemical messengers that regulate the changes during adolescence. The pituitary gland releases hormones that stimulate the testes in boys and ovaries in girls.

üß¥ Personal Hygiene at Adolescence

  • Regular bathing to keep the body clean.
  • Using deodorants to control body odor.
  • Wearing clean clothes.
  • Maintaining dental hygiene.
  • Eating nutritious food for healthy development.

ü§ù Emotional and Social Changes

Adolescents may feel mood swings, desire for independence, and curiosity about opposite sex. Good guidance and understanding from parents and teachers help manage these changes.

üåü Importance of Adolescence

  • Marks the beginning of reproductive capability.
  • Helps develop adult physical features.
  • Shapes mental and emotional maturity.

üß† Key Points to Remember

  • Adolescence is the phase between childhood and adulthood.
  • Physical, emotional, and mental changes happen during this period.
  • Secondary sexual characteristics develop.
  • Menstruation begins in girls.
  • Hormones regulate changes at adolescence.
  • Good personal hygiene is important during adolescence.
