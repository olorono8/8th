üå´Ô∏è What is Pollution?

Pollution means the presence of harmful substances in the environment that cause damage to living beings and nature.

üí® Air Pollution

Air pollution happens when harmful gases, dust, smoke, and chemicals contaminate the air.

  • Common air pollutants include smoke, carbon monoxide, sulfur dioxide, and nitrogen oxides.
  • Sources include vehicles, factories, burning of fossil fuels, and forest fires.
  • Air pollution causes respiratory diseases, allergies, and environmental problems like acid rain and global warming.

üíß Water Pollution

Water pollution occurs when harmful substances enter water bodies, making water unsafe for drinking and harming aquatic life.

  • Sources include sewage discharge, industrial waste, pesticides, and oil spills.
  • Polluted water causes diseases like cholera, dysentery, and typhoid.
  • It also harms fish, plants, and other aquatic organisms.

‚ö†Ô∏è Effects of Pollution

  • Health problems in humans like asthma, bronchitis, and skin diseases.
  • Damage to crops and forests.
  • Contamination of water bodies affecting marine life.
  • Global warming due to greenhouse gases causing climate change.

üõ°Ô∏è Preventing Pollution

  • Use public transport, carpool, or walk to reduce vehicle emissions.
  • Plant more trees to improve air quality.
  • Treat industrial waste before releasing it into water bodies.
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle to minimize waste.
  • Avoid burning plastics and garbage.
  • Raise awareness about pollution and its harmful effects.

‚ôªÔ∏è Role of Individuals

Everyone can contribute to pollution control by adopting eco-friendly habits like conserving water, avoiding plastic, and supporting clean energy.

üåü Key Points to Remember

  • Pollution harms health, environment, and biodiversity.
  • Air pollution is caused by smoke, gases, and dust.
  • Water pollution is caused by sewage, chemicals, and waste.
  • Prevent pollution by reducing waste, using public transport, and planting trees.
  • Individual actions can make a big difference in protecting the environment.
