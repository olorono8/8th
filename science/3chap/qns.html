‚ùì Important Questions and Answers

1. What are natural fibres?

Fibres obtained from plants and animals are called natural fibres. Examples: cotton, wool, silk.

2. What is the difference between fibre and fabric?

Fibre is a thin thread-like structure, while fabric is a material made by weaving or knitting fibres.

3. How is cotton fibre obtained?

Cotton fibre is obtained from the seed hairs of the cotton plant.

4. What is spinning?

Spinning is the process of twisting fibres together to make yarn or thread.

5. What is weaving?

Weaving is the process of interlacing two sets of yarns at right angles to form fabric.

6. What are synthetic fibres?

Fibres made by humans using chemicals are synthetic fibres. Example: nylon, polyester.

7. How is silk obtained?

Silk is obtained from the cocoon of the silkworm.

8. What is sericulture?

Sericulture is the rearing of silkworms for the production of silk.

9. Name two animals other than silkworms that provide fibre.

Goat (mohair fibre), Camel (camel hair).
