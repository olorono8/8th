‚ùì Important Questions and Answers

1. What is combustion?

Combustion is a chemical process in which a substance reacts with oxygen to give off heat and light.

2. Name the types of combustion.

Rapid combustion, spontaneous combustion, and explosive combustion.

3. What is a flame?

A flame is the visible, gaseous part of a fire produced during combustion.

4. What are the different zones of a candle flame?

The dark zone, luminous zone, and non-luminous zone.

5. What is a fuel?

A fuel is a substance that burns to produce heat and energy.

6. What is the calorific value of a fuel?

It is the amount of heat energy produced on complete combustion of 1 kg of fuel, measured in kJ/kg.

7. What is global warming?

It is the increase in Earth's average temperature due to excessive greenhouse gases from burning fuels.

8. What is acid rain?

Rain that contains harmful acids formed by sulfur and nitrogen oxides released during combustion of fuels.

9. What makes a fuel good or ideal?

A good fuel has high calorific value, burns easily, produces less smoke, and is cheap and available.

10. Why is LPG considered a good fuel?

Because it has high calorific value, burns cleanly without smoke, and is easy to store and transport.
